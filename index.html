A Strategic Plan for National Resilience

Building a First Line of Defense for Mental Health in Israel.

ResilientMind is a technology company building **קריאת אמת**, a platform providing immediate, anonymous, and professional support to soldiers, veterans, and families facing trauma. This is our plan to launch, validate, and scale our impact.

The Crisis

A Quantifiable National Emergency

Israel's mental healthcare system is facing a systemic failure, unable to meet the overwhelming demand. This creates a massive, underserved population born from urgent social need.

1 in 5

of the general population is suffering from PTSD and anxiety symptoms following recent national traumas.

3 Years

is the reported wait time for a psychologist in the public system, a catastrophic delay for those in acute distress.

Stigma

in military culture prevents soldiers from seeking help, making anonymous platforms the only viable option for many.

Our Solution

The קריאת אמת App

Our app, **קריאת אמת**, is a fully developed platform ready for launch, designed to dismantle the barriers to mental healthcare.

Immediate & Anonymous Access

Users connect with therapists using a pseudonym, removing the barriers of identity and stigma.

Licensed Israeli Professionals

A network of manually vetted, licensed therapists who understand the cultural and military context.

AI-Powered Support

A clinical AI assistant provides initial emotional support and gathers context for human therapists.

Clinical Tools

Integrated questionnaires (PCL-5, PHQ-9, GAD-7) for evidence-based progress tracking.

"Panic/Hide Mode"

A unique feature to instantly disguise the app, designed for users who lack privacy.

Operational Plan

Founder as CTO (sweat equity), supplemented by specialized contractors. User acquisition will be organic, driven by mission-focused outreach and veteran community partnerships.

Pricing Model

Service will be **free for all users** during Phase 1. This is a strategic investment to rapidly acquire users and therapists motivated by the national mission.

Key Performance Indicators

Success is measured by user/therapist acquisition, session engagement, and **positive clinical outcomes** shown in aggregated, anonymized data from our clinical tools.

Phase 2: The Path to Sustainability

Government & Institutional Contracts (B2G)

Our sole focus for long-term sustainability is to integrate our platform into the national healthcare infrastructure. The clinical data gathered in Phase 1 is the key to unlocking these large-scale, recurring revenue contracts.

**The Pitch:** Approach the Ministry of Defense and HMOs with a cost-effective, scalable, and clinically proven solution.
**The Value:** Offer a turnkey, anonymous solution to offload their overwhelmed systems and treat the "gap population."
**The Model:** Secure stable, recurring revenue through large-scale licensing or per-member-per-month (PMPM) agreements.
